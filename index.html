Our Story

Read more

From year to year, home to home every family has lugged around bulky photo albums or boxes of family photos (pre selfie-era). These precious memories are stored away, hardly touched on a bookshelf, in a closet or in an attic.

My love for organizing "all things" plus my desire for preserving family mementos marked the beginning of my photo scanning business.

I finally decided it was time to "do something" with disregarded family photos because they were not being enjoyed in their designated corner of my dark closet. So, scanning and digitizing the photos onto a thumb-drive and shared site online allowed me to share these photos and enjoy the moments all over again.
